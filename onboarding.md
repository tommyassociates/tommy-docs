This is the full first-run flow — every screen between "I want to try Tommy" and "I have a team running". If you'd rather a five-minute version, see the [Quickstart](/quickstart).

## 1. Sign up

Go to **[app.mytommy.com](https://app.mytommy.com)** and choose **Sign up**.

You can sign up with either an email address or a mobile number. Tommy sends a six-digit code to verify it's really you. Enter the code to continue.

Then add your name and a password. Tommy now has a personal account for you. (You can belong to as many teams as you like under one account.)

## 2. Tell Tommy about your business

Tommy asks three short questions:

- **What industry are you in?** Used to pick a Solution preset
- **Have you used a workforce management system before?** Adjusts how much hand-holding you get
- **How many in your team?** Helps Tommy size the recommendations

Pick the closest match for each. Nothing here is locked in — you can change everything later.

:::tip
If your industry isn't an exact fit, choose **General**. You can swap to a more specific Solution later from team settings.
:::

## 3. Name your workspace and first location

The next screen, **What would you like to name your workspace?**, asks for three things together:

- **Workspace name** — your team name; what members see when they sign in (e.g. "Bluestone Café", "Riverside Disability Support")
- **Location name** — your first physical workplace (e.g. "Main Store", "Head Office", "Bondi")
- **Location address** — used for geofencing if you turn that on later

Tap **Create workspace** to finish. You're now the owner of a team with one location ready to roster against.

## 4. The Get Started checklist

Tommy lands you on a **Get Started** checklist tuned to the Solution you picked. It groups suggested setup tasks into **essentials** and **extras**:

**Essentials** (every team)

- Invite your first team member
- Create a shift
- Install the mobile app

**Extras** (Solution-specific) — for example:

- **NDIS & Care Services**: Add a participant, set up compliance documents, connect Xero
- **Hospitality**: Add your locations, build shift templates for breakfast/lunch/dinner, connect your POS
- **Retail**: Add your stores, set up role-based shift coverage
- **Construction & Trades**: Add a site, set up mileage tracking
- **Staffing Agency**: Add a client, build casual shift templates

Work through the list in any order. Each item links to the screen where you complete it.

## 5. Invite your first team member

The fastest path to seeing Tommy work end-to-end is inviting one person.

Open **Team Members** and tap **Add individual**. Fill in:

- First name, last name
- Email **or** mobile number
- One **Role** from the dropdown (e.g., "Support Worker", "Barista")

Leave **Send invite immediately** ticked and tap **Invite**. Tommy sends them a sign-up link.

For full details — bulk upload, role assignment, locations, documents — see [Team Members](/team-members).

## 6. Publish a first shift

Open **Scheduling**. Tap **New shift**:

- Pick the member you just invited (or leave it open)
- Pick a **Shift Template** to set the times — or set them manually
- Pick a location if you have more than one

Tap **Save shift**, then **Publish**. The shift is now on the member's phone (once they've signed in).

For full details — templates, swaps, open shifts, broadcasts — see [Scheduling](/scheduling).

## 7. Install the mobile app

Tommy works in the browser, but the mobile app is where members spend their time. Search **Tommy** in the App Store or Google Play, sign in with the same account, and you're set.

You can also send members a download link from **Get Started → Install the mobile app**.

## 8. (Optional) Connect your accounting software

If you'll run payroll through Tommy, connect your accounting tool now. From **Settings → Integrations**, choose **Xero**, **QuickBooks**, or **MYOB** and follow the prompts.

Approved timesheets will flow automatically once you've connected.

See [Integrations](/integrations).

## What you've now got

After onboarding, your team has:

- An owner account (you)
- At least one invited member
- A published shift
- A Solution preset shaping the available features

From here, the day-to-day flow is **Scheduling → Time Clock → Timesheets**.
