Tommy is a workforce management platform for teams that work in shifts. Whether you run a disability support service, a café, a retail store, a labour-hire agency, or a building site, Tommy handles the work that surrounds the work — scheduling, time clock, timesheets, compliance, and payroll exports — so you can spend less time on admin and more time on the job.

This documentation walks through every screen and flow in Tommy: how to set up your team, invite members, build rosters, run the time clock, approve timesheets, and connect your accounting software.

## What Tommy does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Scheduling" icon="calendar" href="/scheduling">
    Build shifts from reusable templates. Publish to your team. Manage swaps, drops, and open shifts without text-message chaos.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Time Clock" icon="clock" href="/timeclock">
    Members clock in and out from their phone with GPS and photo verification. Or share a tablet in Kiosk Mode for shared workplaces.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Timesheets" icon="document" href="/timesheets">
    Timesheets build themselves from clocked time. Members submit, managers approve, and payroll exports to your accounting software.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Integrations" icon="link" href="/integrations">
    Connect Xero, QuickBooks, or MYOB to push approved timesheets straight to payroll. POS integrations for hospitality and retail.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
