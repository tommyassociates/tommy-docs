This guide takes you from "I just heard about Tommy" to "my team has shifts on their phones" in five minutes. You'll create your team, pick a Solution, invite a member, and publish a shift.

You'll need an email address and a couple of minutes. That's it.

## 1. Create your account

Go to **[app.mytommy.com](https://app.mytommy.com)** and tap **Sign up**.

Tommy will ask for your email address (or mobile number) and send a verification code. Enter the code, then set your name and a password.

:::note
**Screenshot needed:** Sign-up screen with email field and "Create your free account" heading.
:::

## 2. Create your team

After signing up, Tommy walks you through creating your first **team**. You'll be asked:

<Steps>
  <Step title="What industry are you in?">
    Pick the closest match. This selects your **Solution** — a pre-configured set of roles, shift templates, and features tuned for your industry. Don't stress; you can change it later. See [Solutions](/solutions) for what each one includes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Have you used a workforce management system before?">
    Tells Tommy whether to show extra hints during setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="How many in your team?">
    Helps Tommy size the recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="What would you like to name your workspace?">
    Your team name. This is what your members will see when they sign in. Tap **Create workspace** to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

:::note
**Screenshot needed:** "Tell us a bit about yourself" onboarding form.
:::

## 3. Land on the Get Started checklist

Once your team exists, Tommy shows a **Get Started** checklist with the next things to do — invite members, set up your locations, create shift templates, install the mobile app. Tick them off in any order.

:::note
**Screenshot needed:** Get Started checklist on first team load.
:::

## 4. Invite your first team member

Open **Team Members** and tap **Add individual**.

Enter their first name, last name, and email or mobile number. Pick at least one **Role** (e.g., "Support Worker" or "Barista" — defaults come from your Solution).

Leave **Send invite immediately** ticked, then tap **Invite**. Tommy emails or texts them a sign-up link.

:::tip
If you're inviting a lot of people, use **Bulk upload** with a CSV. See [Team Members](/team-members#bulk-upload).
:::

## 5. Publish your first shift

Open **Scheduling**. You'll see a calendar grid by member or location.

Tap **New shift**:

- Pick the **member** (or leave open for someone to claim)
- Set the **date and time** — or pick a **Shift Template** like "Morning Shift" to fill these for you
- Set the **location** if you have more than one

Tap **Save shift** — it's a draft. To send it to your member's phone, tap **Publish**.

:::note
**Screenshot needed:** Schedule grid with one published shift.
:::

The member receives a notification. They can view the shift, set a reminder, and clock in when the time comes.

## 6. Clock in and out

Your team member opens Tommy on their phone, taps **Clock in**, and Tommy captures the time, location, and (if your Solution requires it) a photo.

When the shift ends, they tap **Clock out**. The hours flow automatically into their **timesheet**.

:::note
**Screenshot needed:** Time Clock screen showing "Clock in" button.
:::

## 7. Approve and export

At the end of the pay period, your member submits their timesheet. You review it, tap **Approve**, and either export to CSV or push it to your accounting software.

Connect Xero, QuickBooks, or MYOB once and approved timesheets flow to payroll automatically. See [Integrations](/integrations).
